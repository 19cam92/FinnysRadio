Finnys Radio is a internet radio streaming program that lets you listen you local radio station
You can also listen to you local emergence service

Keyboard Shortcuts:
*Page Up = Show From Taskbar
*Page Down = Hide To Taskbar
*Home = Music Play
*End = Music Pause
